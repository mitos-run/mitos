## Submitting a notice of alleged infringement

A complete notice should include `TODO(legal): confirm the statutory elements for
the chosen framework`, which under the United States DMCA typically are:

1. a physical or electronic signature of the rights holder or an authorized agent;
2. identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed;
3. identification of the material claimed to be infringing and information
   reasonably sufficient to locate it on the Service;
4. the complainant's contact information;
5. a statement that the complainant has a good-faith belief that the use is not
   authorized; and
6. a statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and
   that the complainant is authorized to act.
